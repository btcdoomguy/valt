## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| BackgroundJobManager → AccountTotalsJob | The scheduler wakes the job based on the public `Interval` property; tampering with this value changes CPU/database wake frequency. |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-15-01 | Tampering | `AccountTotalsJob.Interval` | mitigate | Architecture test `AccountTotalsJob_Interval_Should_Be_At_Least_60_Seconds` fails the build if the interval drops below 60 seconds. |
| T-15-02 | Denial of Service | `AccountTotalsJob.RunAsync` polling loop | mitigate | Interval raised to 120 seconds per D-01, and day-change guard prevents redundant `RefreshCurrentTotalsAsync` calls when the date has not rolled over. |
| T-15-03 | Information Disclosure | Job logs | accept | Logs contain only local dates and "Account totals refreshed" status; no PII, credentials, or account balances are logged. |
</threat_model>
