# Valt

Personal budget management for bitcoiners for Windows and Linux users.

No cloud, no API, no ads, no tracking. Sovereign and free.

Valt is a simple and easy to use budget management app for bitcoiners, since all regular apps and SaaS aren't made for us. With Valt, you can see the most important information about your wealth, all priced in bitcoin.

## Features

- You can create infinite accounts to represent your bitcoin wallets and also your fiat wallets
- Register your spending and income transactions and also your bitcoin transactions
- When transferring between fiat and bitcoin accounts, the app will automatically calculate the exchange rate and the amount in bitcoin terms
- Track a full history of bitcoin and fiat prices paired in USD terms
- Each transaction registered in the app are also calculated in bitcoin terms using the BTC closing price of that day
- Check how much your transaction has cost in bitcoin terms on that specific date and how much it costs now on based on current bitcoin price
- Add all your fixed expenses and keep track of them to avoid missing any payments!
- Shows the current bitcoin price and your favorite fiat currency in real time
- Supports en-US and pt-BR languages
- Supports a wide range of fiat ~~currencies~~
